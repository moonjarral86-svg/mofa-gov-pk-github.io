OUR SERVICES

MOFA attestation, provided by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, is a crucial process for verifying the authenticity of documents, particularly for those intended for use in foreign countries. This service is available both in the UAE and Pakistan, with online options for streamlining the process.

IN THE UAE

MOFA attestation confirms the validity of personal and educational certificates, ensuring they are recognized for official purposes like employment, education, or immigration. The process involves registering on the MOFAIC portal, applying for attestation, and paying the required fees. Fees are typically AED 150 per document, with potential additional courier charges.

IN THE PAKISTAN

MOFA attestation verifies documents such as educational certificates, marriage certificates, birth certificates, and commercial documents. It's a mandatory step for documents intended for use abroad, ensuring they are recognized by foreign authorities. MOFA in Pakistan provides online services for a more convenient and efficient attestation process.

GENERAL INFORMATION

MOFA attestation is a final step in the document legalization process, confirming the authenticity of the seal and signature of the issuing authority. The specific requirements and procedures may vary depending on the country and the type of document.
